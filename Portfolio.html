Title : AUTOMATIC IMAGE AND VIDEO CAPTION

Abstract :

  • Methodologies that utilize Deep Learning offer great potential for applications that automatically attempt to generate captions or descriptions about images and video frames.
  • Image and video captioning are considered to be intellectually challenging problems in imaging science.
  • The application domains include automatic caption (or description) generation for images and videos for people who suffer from various degrees of visual impairment; the automatic creation of metadata for images and videos (indexing) for use by search engines; general-purpose robot vision systems; and many others.
  • Each of these application domains can positively and significantly impact many other task-specific applications.
  • This study treats both image and video captioning by emphasizing the algorithmic overlap between the two.

Introduction :

  • Image processing has played and will continue to play an important role in science and industry.
  • Its applications spread to many areas, including visual recognition and scene understanding , to name a few.
  • Before the advent of Deep Learning, most researchers used imaging methods that worked well on rigid objects in controlled environments with specialized hardware. In recent years, deep learning-based convolutional neural networks have positively and significantly impacted the field of image captioning allowing a lot more flexibility.
  • In this article, we attempt to highlight recent advances in the field of image and video captioning in the context of deep learning.
  • A good description of an image is often said for ’Visualizing a picture in the mind’.
